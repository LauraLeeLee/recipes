Wine Cookies

Makes approx 100 cookies

Ingredients:
-3 tsp baking powder
-5 1/2 - 6 cups flour
-1 egg
-1 cup wine (red or white)
-1 cup sugar
-1/2 cup oil     > or eliminate crisco
-1/2 cup crisco    and use 1 cup oil

Directions:
1. Cream sugar and egg
2. Add wine and oil
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Add flour
5. Roll out pieces of dough into thin cigarette lengths, not
  to thin or thick in diameter.
6. Dip tops in granulated sugar
7. Place on baking sheet
8. Bake at 350Â° for approximately 20 minutes or until bottom edges
   lightly brown.
